OUR STORY


Located in Angeles City, PH is a purveyor of fine Filipino dining from the esteemed Chef Nate Panares.


PHOTOS BY NATE PANARES


RnB Resto Place opened in May 2010 and is located inside the RnB Hotel in Angeles City. Since then, Golden Spork winner Nate Panares has been serving both quality interpretations of classic dishes and daring new ventures into the uncharted territories of food.


RnB Resto Place's interior, designed by the award-winning Hayden Collective, reflects the hip sensibilities of the surrounding area while also paying tribute to Brooklyn's storied past. The multi-level restaurant offers the choice to observe passers-by in the glass-encased Cascade Bar on the 2nd floor, or watch the art of cooking as it occurs in the open-kitchen dining room.
